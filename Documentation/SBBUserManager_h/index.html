An enumeration of the choices for the scope of sharing collected data. 

typedef NS_ENUM(NSInteger, SBBUserDataSharingScope) { 
    SBBUserDataSharingScopeNone = 0, SBBUserDataSharingScopeStudy, 
    SBBUserDataSharingScopeAll 
};
Constants
SBBUserDataSharingScopeNone

The user has not consented to sharing their data.

SBBUserDataSharingScopeStudy

The user has consented only to sharing their de-identified data with the sponsors and partners of the current research study.

SBBUserDataSharingScopeAll

The user has consented to sharing their de-identified data for current and future research, which may or may not involve the same institutions or investigators.
